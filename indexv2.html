Never Not New York began with a feeling that was hard to explain and easy to recognise — building a life somewhere new while still carrying the old one with you.

The story

12,542 km between two homes

It starts the way these things usually do. You move. You memorise a new subway map, learn which train car stops nearest the exit, find your coffee place, and slowly stop converting the prices back into rupees.

And then, on one completely ordinary Tuesday, you miss something impossibly specific. A platform announcement. A vada pav on the walk home. The exact sound of a place that is half a world away.

So we started making products for that feeling — drawn in New York, made by hands in India. Small things that carry a very specific version of home.
